Operating principle

Useful automation should feel controlled, visible, and easy to approve.

We avoid “black box autopilot.” The agent drafts and prepares the work. Your team decides what becomes automatic.

Human approval for sensitive actions

Sending emails, publishing, deleting, charging, or changing data can be gated behind admin confirmation.

Role-based access for public users, team members, and admins.

Clear logs and handover notes so the system is understandable after setup.

Provider-flexible model routing across Claude, OpenAI, DeepSeek, Gemini, or OpenRouter.

Trust & safety

Built for controlled automation.

We keep sensitive actions visible, permissioned, and easy to approve before anything goes live.

Do you need access to private data?

Only the access required for the selected workflow. Credentials can be limited, rotated, and kept outside the public interface.

Can the agent send emails or publish automatically?

Yes, but we recommend starting with human approval. The agent drafts; your team approves before sending, publishing, deleting, or changing data.

Who owns the workflows and prompts?

Your business owns the workflow logic, prompts, documentation, and handover materials created for your setup.

What happens after the MVP?

We review usage, remove weak steps, add integrations, and turn the best manual approvals into safe automations.
