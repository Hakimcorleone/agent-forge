Operating principle

Useful automation should feel controlled, visible, and easy to approve.

We avoid “black box autopilot.” The agent drafts and prepares the work. Your team decides what becomes automatic.

Human approval for sensitive actions

Sending emails, publishing, deleting, charging, or changing data can be gated behind admin confirmation.

Role-based access for public users, team members, and admins.

Clear logs and handover notes so the system is understandable after setup.
